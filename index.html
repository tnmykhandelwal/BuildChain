Operational Workflow

From Registry to Public Audit

01

Project Registry

Admin (Govt/Institution) initializes the project, defining budget, location, and milestones (Foundation → Flooring → Completion) on the ledger.

02

Digital Logging

Contractors log material data (Supplier, Qty, Photos) and labor attendance. Each entry is hashed and permanently stored.

03

Milestone Verification

Upon milestone completion, proof is uploaded. Admins verify quality reports before the system unlocks the next funding stage.

04

Public Audit

Citizens access the read-only dashboard to verify hash chains, view budget utilization charts, and track real-time progress.
